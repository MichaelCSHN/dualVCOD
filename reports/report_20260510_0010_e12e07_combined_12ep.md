2. **R@0.5 is intermediate**: 0.2266 sits between E-12 (0.1961) and E-07 (0.2559) — objectness head drives R@0.5, center loss slightly dampens it
3. **Late upward trajectory**: Unlike E-07 (which peaked at ep11-12 then plateaued for 30ep), combined is still rising at ep11 — suggests it may benefit from extension to 30ep
4. **Oscillation pattern**: Combined shows reduced oscillation compared to E-07 (center loss dampening), but more than E-12 (objectness head adding variance)
5. **R@0.3 lower**: 0.4013 vs 0.465-0.474 for 30ep trials — still early, would improve with more epochs
6. **FPS penalty**: 75.6 vs 80-84 — objectness head has inference overhead, amplified when combined with center loss computation
7. **Zero empty rate maintained**: Both anti-collapse mechanisms (center + objectness) keep predictions non-degenerate
8. **No negative interference**: Losses coexist cleanly — center and objectness gradients don't conflict
