2. **Dataset scope**: Verification covers MoCA (141 videos), MoCA_Mask (71 train videos),
   and CAD (9 animals). Any additional datasets or external video sources are not audited.

3. **Annotation format cross-contamination**: MoCA_Mask and CAD use mask→bbox conversion
   while MoCA uses manual CSV bboxes. The canonical_video_id filter blocks same-source
   visual content but does not protect against annotation format leakage across datasets
   with different canonical names.

4. **Augmentation determinism**: Training uses stochastic augmentation
   (HFlip p=0.5, ColorJitter, StrideJitter). Re-evaluation uses aug=False.
   Results may vary slightly between runs with different random seeds.

5. **Model capacity**: MicroVCOD with 1.4M params and MobileNetV3-Small backbone is a
   lightweight model. Performance ceiling may be limited by model capacity, not data.

6. **This report verifies data isolation correctness, NOT model performance claims.**
   The mIoU=0.2862 and R@0.5=0.1978 are clean, honest metrics obtained under strict
   data isolation. They should NOT be compared to the leaked 0.8705 mIoU — that
   value was inflated by training on val data and is permanently invalid.
